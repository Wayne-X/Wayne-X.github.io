Introduction

Hello, my name is Wayne Xun. I am an undergraduate student at Northwestern University where I study Computer Science as a rising Junior. I am also pursuing an Electrical Engineering minor and a Segal Design Certificate.

I find Computer Science equal parts challenging and rewarding. I've found myself stuck on completely bizarre bugs sometimes, but time and time again coding has shown me that it's worth it to stick around past the frustration to see what you can learn and the incredible self growth that comes from it.

As a soon to be college graduate, I'm eager to find out as much as possible about what types of projects and people there are out there to work on and get to know. To that end, please feel free to reach out with any advice and guidance that you may have for a curious student.
